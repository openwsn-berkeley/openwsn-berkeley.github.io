18 /* NOTE: There are more DMA requests than DMA channels, so

20  * assignments are unique for the modules that are
21  * being used at any time.

24  * register before updating it to verify it is 0x0. If the
25  * DMAMUX_CHCFGn register is not zero, then that indicates the
26  * selected DMA channel might already be in use by another peripheral
27  * (a more specific test would be to look for DMAMUX_CHCFGn[ENBL] set).
